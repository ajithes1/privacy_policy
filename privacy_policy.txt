# Privacy Policy for Wealthora AI

**Effective Date:** 31-03-2026

Wealthora AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

---

## 📱 Overview

Wealthora AI is a **fully offline personal finance application** designed to help users track expenses, manage income, and monitor financial activity.

We prioritize **user privacy and data security**. The app is built to function **without collecting or transmitting personal data**.

---

## 🔒 Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.**

* No account registration required
* No personal information is collected
* No financial data is sent to any server
* No tracking or analytics tools are used

All data entered in the app (such as expenses, income, categories, and notes) is stored **locally on your device only**.

---

## 💾 Data Storage

All your data is:

* Stored locally on your device
* Not accessible by us or any third party
* Fully under your control

If you uninstall the app, your data may be permanently deleted unless backed up by your device.

---

## 🌐 Internet Usage

Wealthora AI works completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

The app does not transmit any data over the internet.

---

## 🔐 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure your data is secure on your device. However, since data is stored locally, users are responsible for:

* Securing their device
* Using device-level protections (PIN, biometrics, etc.)

---

## 👶 Children’s Privacy

Wealthora AI does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.
